360 Wellness Physical Therapy – SMS Appointment Notifications

By providing your mobile phone number to 360 Wellness Physical Therapy during your visit, over the phone, or through our online booking system, you consent to receive text messages regarding:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Rescheduling notifications
  • Urgent updates related to your treatment schedule

Frequency: No more than 2–3 messages per appointment.
Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying “STOP”.

We value your privacy. Your phone number will not be used for marketing purposes or shared with third parties.

中文

360 Wellness 物理治疗诊所 – 短信通知说明

您在现场预约、电话预约，或通过我们的网站填写预约信息时所提供的手机号码，即表示您同意我们通过短信方式发送如下信息：

  • 预约确认与提醒
  • 修改预约时间通知
  • 与您治疗时间相关的紧急通知

发送频率：每次预约最多 2–3 条提醒短信。
可能会产生标准短信或数据费用。您可随时回复 “STOP” 退订短信提醒服务。

我们重视您的隐私。您的手机号码不会被用于市场营销，也不会分享给第三方。
